Egg source

This one is going to be easy. If you are new to making Nigerian foods and desires to start with the recipes that are easy, I would suggest you start here. This takes just about six different ingredients and the preparation time is less than 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 2 spoons sliced pepper
  • Vegetable oil (200ml).
  • 2 cups of sliced fresh tomatoes.
  • Sliced pepper to taste.
  • 1 cup of sliced onions.
  • One cube of maggi or knorr.
  • Salt to taste.

Steps

  1. Start by Slicing your tomatoes, onions, and peppers using a kitchen knife.
  2. Set frying pan on the heat and add just 200ml of oil (vegetable or olive oil), allow the oil to heat up for few minutes.
  3. Add the sliced tomatoes, peppers, onions and fry.
  4. Add salt to taste, half of a seasoning cube. Stir to avoid burning.
  5. Beat eggs in a separate bowl, add a pinch of salt and pour it into the frying tomatoes.
  6. Allow the egg to fry.
  7. Don't let it get burned. Try breaking into tiny crumbs so you can get an exact replica of what you see here.
  8. Serve egg sauce with boiled white yams.
